# Changelog

## 0.1.0

Initial release.

- Browser-only FASTA and FASTQ exact deduplication.
- Deduplication by sequence content, ID, or full record.
- Case, whitespace, gap, sequence type, and reverse-complement settings.
- Representative selection by first occurrence, longest sequence, longest header, FASTQ mean quality, or deterministic rename.
- Deduplicated FASTA/FASTQ export.
- Mapping, duplicate group, count, summary, reproducibility JSON, and methods exports.
- Built-in demo data and validation tests.
- Announcement blog page and tutorials.
